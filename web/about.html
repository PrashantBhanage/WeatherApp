Who We Are?

AQI was built on a simple but powerful idea — everyone deserves to know what they are breathing. Understanding air quality is the first step toward protecting your health and living a cleaner, safer life.

We are an open-source air quality monitoring platform that provides real-time air quality data for your location. AQI doesn’t just show numbers — it helps you understand what those numbers mean and how they affect your daily life.

Our platform allows you to track air pollution levels in your city, monitor air quality in homes, offices, and workplaces, and stay informed about changing environmental conditions.

Along with real-time insights, AQI also offers practical guidance and preventive plans to help you reduce exposure to air pollution.

For users of Prana Air monitors, AQI provides a dedicated dashboard that makes it easy to analyze, visualize, and manage air quality data from connected devices — all in one place.

At its core, AQI exists to empower people with information, awareness, and tools — because clean air should never be a mystery.
